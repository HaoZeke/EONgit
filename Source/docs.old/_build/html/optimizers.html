OptimizersÂ¶

The eon client provides two optimizers quick-min and conjugate gradients. These options are in the [Optimizers] section.

max_iterations:

Default: 1000

The maximum number of optimization iterations that the will be performed.

converged_force:

Default: 0.005

When the maximum force (in eV/A) on any one atom is smaller than this value, the structure is considered minimized.

max_move:

Default: 0.2

Maximum distance that an atom may be moved in a single optimization step.

time_step:

Default: 0.1

The dynamical timestep for the quickmin algorithm.

finite_diff_step:

Default: 0.001

The finite difference step size.
